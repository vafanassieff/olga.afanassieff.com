Young, enthusiastic, flexible and motivated graphic designer seeking new opportunities in the digital creative sector. Always keen to work on different projects and explore new opportunities, please feel reach to out to me.

Who am I ?

After completing a Bachelor in Information and Communication, I have graduated from a Master in Digital Design and Art Direction at Sup de Pub Paris while working as a multimedia graphic assistant at the “Monnaie de Paris” from March 2016 to July 2017.

From an early age, I have always been drawn to the beauty of the world. My passion for the Design and Digital Art field goes beyond a scholar interest: creating, imagining and designing are essential pillars of my everyday life.
This doesn’t come as a surprise my father being a professional mountaineer as well as a documentary director. I have therefore always been pushed to see and appreciate the beauty of the world that surrounds us.
My childhood as also fostered my thirst for adventure, discovery and travelling. I have lived in France and Malta and backpacked through Mexico, the USA and Europe. During these trips I cherished the most the outstanding landscapes and the human interactions I created. Having grown-up in Chamonix Mont-Blanc I am passionate about skiing and yoga.

Thanks to my professional and personal experiences I have convinced this field is what I am made to do.
