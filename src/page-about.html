About me.

Olga Afanassieff, Graphic Designer.

My name is Olga and I come from Chamonix Mont-Blanc in France, I'm a graphic designer based in Berlin. I am a true passionate of design. After completing a Bachelor in Information and Communication specilized in Digital Art, I have graduated from a Master in Digital Design and Art Direction at Sup de Pub Paris while working as a multimedia graphic design at the “Monnaie de Paris” from March 2016 to July 2017, then after I worked as a freelancer for the FACCSF (French-American Chamber of Commerce of San Francisco), Piltons Working men's club (Glastonbury Festival) and other...
In my free time I enjoy be on the road and spending time with my dog, read about philosiphy. My other passions are yoga and photography.

Feel free to contact me for inqueries or just to say hello :)
