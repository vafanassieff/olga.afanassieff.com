About me.

Olga, Graphic Designer.

Hi, I’m Olga, a passionate graphic designer from the picturesque Chamonix Mont Blanc in France,
now thriving in Berlin. Currently, I’m crafting visually striking and impactful designs as part of the global team at HelloFresh.
With a Bachelor's in Information and Communication (specializing in Digital Art) and a Master's in Digital Design and Art Direction from Sup de Pub Paris, I’ve honed my creative expertise across diverse industries. From cultural endeavors at Monnaie de Paris to driving innovation in industrial water and energy management at Veolia Deutschland, and even shaping sustainable fashion at the textile startup Ooia, each experience has enriched my perspective. Since 2021, my focus has been on digital design, mastering areas like CRM, Display, OOH, and Paid Social, while leveraging cutting-edge platforms such as Celtra and Smartly. Beyond the screen, you’ll often find me exploring new horizons, reconnecting with nature alongside my loyal four-legged companion, or challenging myself through yoga and bouldering.
Let’s connect and create something extraordinary together!

Feel free to contact me for inqueries or just to say hello :)
Olga
